## What is SolarSlice?

SolarSlice is an iOS app for residents of buildings with an Allume Energy SolShare installation. It connects to the SolCentre API to display your unit's solar consumption, grid import, and export data.

## Requirements

- An active SolCentre account (provided by your building manager or Allume Energy)
- An Allume Energy SolShare installation at your property

## Troubleshooting

**I can't log in**
Use the same email and password as the SolCentre customer portal (solcentre.allumeenergy.com). If you've forgotten your password, reset it there first.

**Data isn't loading or is out of date**
Pull down on the main screen to refresh. Data is sourced from the SolCentre API and is typically available within a few minutes of each interval closing. If the API is unavailable, try again shortly.

**The widget isn't updating**
Open the app and let it load — the widget is populated from data fetched by the main app. It does not refresh independently in the background.

**I don't see my property**
If your account has multiple properties, you will be prompted to select one on first launch. If your property is missing, contact your building manager to confirm your SolCentre account is correctly provisioned.

## Privacy

SolarSlice does not collect or transmit any personal data. Your login credentials are stored securely in the iOS Keychain on your device. See [PRIVACY.md](PRIVACY.md) for full details.
